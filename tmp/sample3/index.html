Event Zones

Discover six distinct zones designed to spark curiosity and creativity.

STEM Zone

Hands-on experience with Robotics, AI, AR/VR, Drones, and Astronomy.

Science Zone

Live experiments and demos from Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs.

Arts Zone

Painting exhibitions, Elocution, Bracelet making, and Documentaries.

Innovation Zone

Showcasing cutting-edge products from industry partners and local startups.

Student Zone

Innovative projects displayed by students from 85+ participating schools.

Book Zone

A comprehensive collection of books to inspire young curious minds.

Our Vision for 2026

Building on the massive success of Pragyaan 2025, we are scaling up to reach even more young minds.

With a target of over 5000 students and 85+ schools, Pragyaan 2026 aims to be the largest open-day event in the region.
